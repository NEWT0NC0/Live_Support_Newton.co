What are Networks?

Networks are blockchain platforms that process transactions. Common options include:

  • Ethereum: The most widely used, supports ERC-20 tokens.
  • Binance Smart Chain (BSC): A faster, cheaper alternative to Ethereum.
  • Tron: Known for low fees and fast transactions.
  • Polygon: A Layer 2 solution built on Ethereum for scalability.
